renowned local chip shop

This popular local chip shop wanted their very own platform to showcase their menu and extend their reach into the wider community. Previously they mainly relied on word of mouth and their facebook page to advertise their business.

challenge:

Create a responsive site that would be easy to navigate (especially for the elderly), showcase menu and contact details. The client also requested a section to give some insight into the shops history and community work.

solution:

Keeping those less technology savy in mind, I opted for a simple layout on a one page format for ease of access.

technology:

HTML, Sass, JavaScript & Figma

outcome:

This website is easy to navigate whether being viewed on a mobile or desktop.
